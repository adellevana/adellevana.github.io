Journaling for Mental Health

Journaling is said to be beneficial for Mental Health - an important and serious condition often overlooked. It is said to help with these mental health aspects:


  • 1. Anxiety
  • 2. Stress
  • 3. Depression


Journaling helps one navigate their presence in reality more clearly. It allows the writer to be more concious towards their actions and interactions with the world, the people and themselves. It helps the writer see a clearer picture of their day-to-day lives, which in turn, contributes to a better judgement.


In one study, Journaling is found to have effectively done the followi ng to people:


  • 1. Clarify thoughts
  • 2. Understand oneself
  • 3. Stress management
  • 4. Problem solving
  • 5. Improves mood


Writing helps release troubled thoughts and emotions. Writing periodically, journaling, is therapeutic and beneficial for mental health. Each time someone writes something negative, writing drains it away and leaves one with the reflection of what they are inside. It helps the writer evaluate themselves better when everything is laid out in front of them.
